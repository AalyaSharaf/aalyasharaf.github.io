What is the 30MFF?

The 30 Minute Film Festival, or 30MFF, is a project in which you are required to record and edit a short film within the span of 30 minutes. It does not matter how long your film is or what the topic is, as long as all the work is done withing the 30 minute time limit!

What is our Video About?

The main concept of our group's film was coffee! The inspiration itself came from our surroundings. Being near a coffee shop, there were naturally lots of students with coffee cups. The aim was to try to get an identical shot of them drinking their coffees and then putting together all the snapshots to form one cohesive video.
